FPV Drone Consumables and Essential Equipment ​

When building a quadcopter, you'll need tools, spare parts, and various consumables. The list below covers the essentials for FPV drones. You can also find shopping lists for FPV airplanes and flying wings on this site. The links provided are from previous orders; if a link is broken or you want to save money, use the product names or images to search for alternatives. If you find a dead link, please open an issue.
