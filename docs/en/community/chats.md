# Well-known FPV and Aviation Chats and Channels

## Chat Rules

### General Rules for DRS Groups:

Profanity, insults, and anything prohibited by law are forbidden in the groups. Off-topic discussion is highly discouraged and may be classified as a violation under certain conditions. Bans are at the discretion of the admins, ranging from 24 hours to permanent.
The use of clones is prohibited. If a clone is added, all suspects will be permanently banned.
Usernames consisting of only one or two characters or other unidentifiable names are prohibited. It's best to use your real first and last name. If you're concerned about privacy, come up with something that uniquely identifies you.
We ask everyone to add an avatar!

### Recommendations for Asking Questions

First and foremost, the community dislikes meta-questions (more details at [nometa.xyz](https://nometa.xyz/en.md)).

If you expect detailed answers and useful advice from the community, it is recommended to describe your problems and ideas as thoroughly as possible, including steps taken, versions of equipment and firmware used, and other details that might have occurred. Keep in mind that hobbyists in the chat are not a support service; they may choose to answer or ignore you. Occasionally you may encounter trolls; try not to pay attention to their jokes to avoid conflict and sanctions. Do not ignore follow-up questions, as they can help resolve your situation.

Don't hesitate to ask clarifying questions, but note that if your question is easily found in a search engine, you might be ignored or told to "Google it." To avoid frustrating people and appearing like someone who cannot use internet resources, try searching in a browser or even within the chat using keywords first.

If you don't know how to describe something, try referring to the [glossary](/en/intro/glossarium).

Below are general and thematic chats. If you have a question about something specific, it's more useful to contact a thematic chat if one exists in the list below.
